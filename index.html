Japan??

I like to think of Japan as a place where it's like New York, but MUCH cheaper and the people there are much nicer than most people here. There may not be as many monuments of history in the city part of Japan, but there sure is history when you go out and look at all the museums that contain its history. When you think of the history of Japan or even Japan in general, you think of samurai with katanas. There are many museums in Japan about samurai and ninjas that not only go through the history, but it's also a type of shopping district when you go there.

While you're there, I've always been jealous of how good the food is there and how CHEAP it is. I've only heard amazing things about the food that people get just at a convenience store. Don't even talk to me about the 7/11 there. The food sold there is absolutely amazing compared to the food sold at the 7/11s in America. You won't even want to go back to America after eating the food there because the food here will just taste like absolutely nothing.

Just from hearing the experiences of my friends who went there, it just makes me want to go so bad. And it doesn't help that I'm an anime fan. Anime originates from Japan so it's definitely going to be an experience for me especially because of my love for anime. So going there in the spring is something I've been waiting for since this past summer. Everything there just fits most of my hobbies besides opera which is a bummer, but it's a really good place to just enjoy yourself and experience new things. I also can't wait to take pictures of the amazing scenery there with all the beautiful flowers and mountains there.
